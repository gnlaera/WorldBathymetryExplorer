What changed in this reviewed build

  • Uses the attached file's stronger Leaflet plus on-demand Cesium pattern.
  • Retains Mercator, Plate Carree, and 3D globe frames.
  • Splits bathymetry, depth contours, and nautical overlays into separate opacity controls.
  • Moves numeric point queries to a resilient three-stage stack: NOAA/NCEI DEM sample, GEBCO WMS feature query, then ETOPO ERDDAP fallback.
  • Removes direct Google tile URLs. Google map tiles require keyed Google Maps Platform access.

Bathymetry

GEBCO WMS latest layers provide global shaded relief, colour elevation, measured-data, and sub-ice/topography visual layers. NOAA/NCEI services provide BAG high-resolution bathymetry where coverage exists, the global DEM mosaic and ETOPO fallback queries.

Contours and nautical overlays

Global GEBCO-derived contour tiles, OpenSeaMap seamarks and depth contours, Esri Ocean reference labels, EMODnet regional bathymetry, NOAA chart display services for United States waters, the NOAA/NCEI high-resolution BAG bathymetry mosaic, and an experimental ArgoGIS ENC overlay.

Point readout

Click or tap anywhere. The readout now tries NOAA BAG first where available, then NOAA/NCEI global DEM, GEBCO GetFeatureInfo, and ETOPO. Negative values are interpreted as depth below the vertical datum. Positive values are land, ice, or topographic elevation.
