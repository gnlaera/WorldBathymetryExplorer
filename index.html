Data and use

Sources include GEBCO, NOAA/NCEI DEM, NOAA ETOPO, NOAA BAG, OpenSeaMap, Esri Ocean, NOAA chart/ENC, EMODnet, AusSeabed and USGS/Esri tectonic boundary data. Click the map for a point probe. Use Profile to pick two points and draw a sampled sea-level cross-section. Operational overlays are off by default except the GEBCO bathymetry layer; enable contours, nautical layers and tectonics from Layers or presets. Negative values are below datum; positive values are elevation.

Operational caveats

Bathymetry, contour and tectonic layers are screening layers only. Vertical datums, grid resolution, update cycles and coverage vary by source. Tectonic boundaries are approximate and are not suitable for hazard assessment, navigation, engineering design or legal use.
